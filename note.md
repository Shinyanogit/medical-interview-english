## 主訴の聴取 (Chief Complaint - Open Question)

- "What brings you in (here) today?"
- "What can I help you with (assist you with, do for you) today?"
- "How can I help (assist) you today?"

## OPQRST (症状聴取の基本フレームワーク)

### O: Onset (発症)

- "When did the symptoms start (begin)? Was it sudden (abrupt, acute) or gradual (slow, progressive)?"
- "What were you doing when the symptoms began (started, occurred)?"
- "Were there any events or activities that preceded (came before) the symptoms?"

### P: Palliative (Alleviating) & Provocative (Aggravating) factors (増悪・寛解因子)

- "Is there anything that makes the symptoms better (improved) or worse (aggravated)?"
- "Does rest, activity, or certain positions affect (influence, change) the symptoms?"
- "Have you tried anything that helped relieve (ease, alleviate) the symptoms?"

### Q: Quality (性状)

- "How would you describe (characterize) the symptom? For example, is it sharp (stabbing), dull (aching), burning, or aching (throbbing)?"
- "Does the symptom occur (happen) with exertion (physical activity, exercise) or at rest?"
- "Is the symptom affected (influenced) by changes in position?"

### R: Region and Radiation (部位と放散)

- "Where exactly is the symptom located (situated)? Can you point to (show me) it?"
- "Does the symptom spread (radiate, extend) or move to any other areas (places, locations)?"
- "Is the symptom localized (confined, limited) to one area, or does it affect a wider region (larger area)?"

### S: Severity & Associated Symptoms (重症度と随伴症状)

- "On a scale from 1 (no pain) to 10 (worst pain imaginable), how severe (intense, bad) is it?"
- "How much does this symptom interfere with (disrupt, affect) your daily activities (routine, life)?"
- "Are there any other symptoms that come along with (accompany) this, such as nausea (feeling sick), sweating, or shortness of breath (difficulty breathing)?"

### T: Time course (時間経過)

- "How long does the symptom last (persist, continue) when it occurs (happens)?"
- "Is the symptom constant (continuous, ongoing), or does it come and go (intermittent, episodic)?"
- "Is there any particular (specific) time of day when the symptom is better or worse?"

## 相槌・共感表現 (Empathetic Responses & Acknowledgments)

### 共感を示す (Showing empathy)

- "I'm sorry to hear that."
- "That must be tough (difficult, hard, challenging) for you."
- "That sounds really challenging (difficult, tough)."

### 理解を示す (Showing understanding)

- "I understand (see, got it)."
- "I hear what you're saying."
- "That makes sense."

### 励まし・安心させる (Reassurance)

- "I'm here to help you."
- "We'll work through this together."
- "Thank you for sharing (telling me) that."

### 傾聴を示す (Active listening)

- "Please, go on. I'm listening."
- "Tell me more about that."
- "Is there anything else you'd like to add?"

## 便・尿・体重・食欲・睡眠(流れで聞きたい)

**便 (Bowel movements):**

- "How often (frequently) do you have bowel movements (BMs, go to the bathroom)?"
- "Have you noticed (observed, seen) any changes in your bowel habits, such as diarrhea or constipation?"
- "Is there any blood or unusual (abnormal) color in your stool (feces, poop)?"

**尿 (Urination):**

- "How often (frequently) do you urinate (pass urine, pee) during the day and night?"
- "Do you have any pain, burning sensation (discomfort), or difficulty (trouble) when urinating?"
- "Have you noticed (observed) any changes in the color or smell (odor) of your urine?"

**体重 (Weight):**

- "Have you experienced (had, noticed) any weight loss or weight gain recently (lately)?"
- "If so, how much weight have you lost or gained, and over what period of time (time frame, duration)?"
- "Was this weight change intentional (on purpose, planned) or unintentional (unplanned, unexpected)?"

**食欲 (Appetite):**

- "How is your appetite (hunger)? Have you noticed (observed) any changes?"
- "Are you eating less or more than usual (normal, typical)?"
- "Do you have any difficulty (trouble, problems) swallowing or nausea (feeling sick) after eating?"

**睡眠 (Sleep):**

- "How many hours of sleep do you get (have) per night (each night)?"
- "Do you have any difficulty (trouble, problems) falling asleep or staying asleep (sleeping through the night)?"
- "Do you feel well-rested (refreshed, energized) when you wake up in the morning?"

## か：家族歴 (Family History)

- "Is there any history of serious (major, significant) illnesses in your family, such as heart disease, diabetes, or cancer?"
- "Are your parents still alive? If not, what did they pass away from (die from, cause of death)?"
- "Do any of your siblings (brothers or sisters) or close relatives (family members) have similar symptoms or conditions?"

## き：既往歴 (Past Medical History)

- "Have you had any major (serious, significant) illnesses, surgeries (operations), or hospitalizations in the past (previously, before)?"
- "Do you have any chronic (long-term, ongoing) medical conditions, such as diabetes, hypertension (high blood pressure), or asthma?"
- "Have you ever been diagnosed (told by a doctor) with this condition before (in the past)?"

## く：薬 (Medications)

- "Are you currently (now, at present) taking any medications (medicines, drugs), including over-the-counter drugs or supplements?"
- "Can you tell me (let me know) the names and dosages (doses, amounts) of your medications?"
- "Do you have any allergies (allergic reactions) to medications (medicines)?"

## け：健康診断 (Health Screenings)

- "When was your last health check-up (physical exam, medical checkup) or physical examination?"
- "Have you had any recent (latest) blood tests, X-rays, or other screening tests?"
- "Were there any abnormal (unusual, concerning) findings (results) in your recent health screenings?"

## こ：対処行動 (Coping Behaviors / Travel History)

- "Have you done anything to treat (help, address) or manage (handle, deal with) these symptoms?"
- "Have you tried (used, attempted) any home remedies or alternative treatments (therapies)?"
- "Have you traveled abroad (overseas, internationally) recently (lately)? If so, where and when?"

## さ：酒タバコ (Alcohol and Tobacco - 量と期間を聴取)

**飲酒 (Alcohol):**

- "Do you drink alcohol? If so, how often (frequently) and how much (what amount) do you drink?"
- "How many years have you been drinking (consuming alcohol)?"
- "What type of alcohol do you usually (typically, normally) drink, and how many drinks per day or week?"

**喫煙 (Tobacco):**

- "Do you smoke? If so, how many cigarettes do you smoke per day (daily)?"
- "How many years have you been smoking, or when did you quit (stop)?"
- "Have you ever tried (attempted) to quit (stop) smoking?"

## し：仕事 (Work / Occupation)

- "What do you do for a living (occupation, job)?"
- "Does your work involve (require, include) any physical labor (manual work) or exposure to chemicals or hazardous (dangerous) materials?"
- "How stressful is your job, and does it affect (impact, influence) your health?"

## す：ストレス・睡眠 (Stress and Sleep)

**ストレス (Stress):**

- "Have you been under a lot of stress (pressure, strain) recently (lately)?"
- "What are the main (primary, major) sources (causes) of stress in your life right now (currently)?"
- "How do you usually (typically, normally) cope with (handle, manage, deal with) stress?"

**睡眠 (Sleep):**

- "How would you describe (characterize) the quality of your sleep?"
- "Do you snore or has anyone noticed (observed) you stop breathing during sleep?"
- "Do you take any medications or supplements to help you sleep (fall asleep, sleep better)?"

## せ：生理・性生活 (Menstruation and Sexual History)

**生理 (Menstruation - for female patients):**

- "When was the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP, last period)?"
- "Are your periods regular (normal, consistent)? How many days does your cycle last (take, continue)?"
- "Do you experience (have) any pain, heavy bleeding, or other symptoms during your period?"

**性生活 (Sexual History):**

- "Are you currently (now, at present) sexually active?"
- "Do you use any form of contraception (birth control) or protection?"
- "Have you ever been diagnosed with or treated for sexually transmitted infections (STIs, STDs)?"

## 患者の考え (Patient's Perspective)

- "What do you think is causing (leading to, responsible for) your symptoms?"
- "Do you have any concerns (worries, fears) or worries about your condition?"
- "Is there anything specific (particular) you're worried (concerned) about?"

## まとめ (Summary)

- "Let me summarize (sum up, review) what we've discussed (talked about) today."
- "So, to recap (summarize, in summary), you've been experiencing..."
- "Just to make sure I understand (comprehend) correctly, let me go over (review, summarize) what you've told me."
